How to Play

  • Players take turns to roll the dice and move their token.
  • If you land on the bottom of a ladder, climb up to the top!
  • If you land on the head of a snake, slide down to its tail.
  • The first player to reach square 100 wins the game.
  • You must roll the exact number to land on 100.
